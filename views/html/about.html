About

Discover our story

At NutriVue, we believe in empowering individuals to make informed decisions about the food they consume. Our platform is built on the robust Open Food Facts API, a collaborative database of food products from around the world. This partnership allows us to access a wealth of data, including detailed information about ingredients, nutritional values, and other critical product details.

By leveraging the Open Food Facts API, we’ve created an intuitive application that not only helps users access this data but also enables them to contribute back to the database. Every time you interact with NutriVue, you're not just gaining insight into healthier eating — you’re becoming part of a global effort to enhance the transparency and accessibility of food information. Our goal is to make food data available and actionable, so everyone can make healthier and more conscious choices.

Currently, our platform utilizes Version 2 of the API, ensuring we’re working with the most up-to-date and comprehensive information available. Whether you’re scanning a barcode, exploring ingredient lists, or learning about nutritional facts, NutriVue is here to guide you toward better eating habits — one product at a time.
